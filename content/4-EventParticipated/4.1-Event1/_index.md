# Event 1 – REMINDER FCAJ Community Day

### Event Information

- **Event Name:** REMINDER FCAJ Community Day
- **Date & Time:** 09:00 AM, May 30, 2026
- **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, No. 2 Hai Trieu Street, Sai Gon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
- **Role:** Participant

---

### Purpose of the Event

- Introduce the FCAJ community and its activities.
- Provide information about the AWS Hackathon program.
- Introduce AWS Game 3D and the FloCI learning platform.
- Create opportunities for networking, knowledge sharing, and community engagement among AWS enthusiasts.

---

### Main Activities

#### Learning about AWS Hackathon

- Introduction to the AWS Hackathon program.
- Overview of its objectives, participation process, and common competition topics.
- Sharing experiences and preparation strategies for Cloud Computing competitions.

#### Exploring AWS Game 3D

- Introduction to the AWS Game 3D learning platform.
- Demonstration of learning AWS services through interactive simulation scenarios.
- Exploring practical ways to improve hands-on cloud skills.

#### Discovering FloCI

- Introduction to the FloCI cloud learning platform.
- Overview of learning materials, online courses, and hands-on laboratories.
- Guidance on building an effective AWS learning roadmap.

#### Networking with the Community

- Exchanging experiences in learning and using AWS services.
- Discussing cloud learning pathways and career development.
- Connecting with community members to expand professional networks and gain practical insights.

---

### Results and Benefits

After participating in the event, I gained the following benefits:

- A better understanding of the FCAJ community and AWS ecosystem.
- Knowledge about AWS Hackathon and how to prepare for future cloud competitions.
- Exposure to learning platforms such as AWS Game 3D and FloCI to enhance both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
- Valuable insights from community members regarding AWS learning, certification preparation, and career development.
- Opportunities to expand my professional network with people who share similar interests in Cloud Computing.

---

### Application to My Internship

- Apply the knowledge gained to build a more effective AWS learning roadmap.
- Continue studying and practicing AWS services to support the development of the **Security Operations Center (SOC)** project.
- Participate actively in AWS community events to stay updated with new technologies and industry trends.
- Prepare myself for future AWS Hackathons and other cloud-related competitions.

---

### Personal Experience

Participating in **REMINDER FCAJ Community Day** gave me an excellent opportunity to connect with the AWS community and discover various learning resources designed for students interested in Cloud Computing.

Through the sessions introducing **AWS Hackathon**, **AWS Game 3D**, and **FloCI**, I learned about different ways to improve my cloud knowledge and practical skills. The networking session was equally valuable, allowing me to exchange experiences with community members regarding AWS certifications, learning strategies, and personal cloud projects.

Overall, the event broadened my understanding of the AWS ecosystem, inspired me to continue developing my cloud expertise, and motivated me to become more involved in the AWS community throughout my internship.
